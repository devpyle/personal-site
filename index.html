01 About

Most of my career has been in financial services and digital banking, but my core strengths travel well: API product strategy, system integration, platform modernization, and cross-functional delivery in regulated, high-stakes environments.

I like the messy middle: contracts, schemas, auth flows, and the cross-team coordination it takes to ship them without breaking what customers depend on.
